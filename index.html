Carolyn Wu is a data analyst passionate about the intersection of technology, healthcare, and social inequities. She believes that human-centered design and evidenced based decision making can transform the public health sphere. A recent University of Michigan graduate with a B.S. in Informatics, she hopes to work in government to address systemic inequalities. In her free time, Carolyn enjoys photography, thrifting, and playing very bad chess.
